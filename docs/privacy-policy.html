Privacy Policy

Jahera – Build Better Habits

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Welcome to Jahera ("we", "our", or "us"), operated by Tumdah (admin@tumdah.com). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use the Jahera mobile application ("App"), how we use it, and what rights you have over it.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

1. Who This App Is For

Jahera is a parenting and family habit-formation tool intended for use by adults (parents and guardians). The App allows parents to create personalised AI-generated stories as a habit-building ritual for their children. Parents control all accounts and provide any information about their children on behalf of those children.

We do not knowingly allow children to create their own accounts or use the App independently. All data pertaining to a child (such as their name or age) is entered by a parent or guardian and remains under that parent's control.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Email address – used to create and manage your account
  • Password – stored as a secure hash; we never see your plain-text password

2.2 Child Profile Information (Parent-Provided)

To personalise stories, parents optionally provide:

  • Child's first name
  • Child's age
  • City, region, and country (to contextualise story settings)
  • Profile avatar image (uploaded from the device's photo library)
  • Preferred language for story narration
  • Selected voice preferences (tone, style)

This information is stored securely on our servers and is used solely to generate personalised story content. It is never sold or shared for advertising purposes.

2.3 Generated Content

  • AI-generated stories created within the App
  • Audio narrations generated for those stories
  • Story playback history and progress

2.4 Subscription & Purchase Information

Subscription management is handled by RevenueCat. We receive anonymised subscription status and entitlement data (e.g., whether you are a free or premium subscriber). We do not process or store your payment card details — these are handled entirely by the App Store / Google Play and RevenueCat.

2.5 Device & Technical Information

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • Push notification token (only if you grant permission for reminders)
  • Crash logs and error diagnostics

We do not collect precise GPS location. City and country information is only used as story context and is entered manually by the parent.

Jahera uses the following trusted third-party providers to deliver its features. Each has its own privacy policy and data processing terms.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. Specifically:

  • Account data – retained until you delete your account
  • Child profile data – retained within your account until you delete the profile or account
  • Generated stories & audio – retained in your library until you delete them or close your account
  • Crash logs – automatically purged after 90 days

Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently erased within 30 days, except where retention is required by applicable law.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Hashed and salted password storage
  • Access controls limiting who can view user data
  • Regular security reviews of our backend infrastructure

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

Jahera is operated by and for parents and guardians. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. All information about a child entered into the App is provided by a parent or guardian who takes full responsibility for that submission.

If you believe a child has independently registered an account or submitted personal information without parental consent, please contact us immediately at admin@tumdah.com and we will promptly delete that information.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access – request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction – update inaccurate or incomplete data (via in-app settings)
  • Deletion – request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability – request your data in a portable, machine-readable format
  • Objection – object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent – disable push notifications at any time in your device settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at admin@tumdah.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. International Data Transfers

Jahera is a global service. Your data may be processed in countries outside your own, including the United States, where our third-party providers (Appwrite, RevenueCat, ElevenLabs) operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including standard contractual clauses and provider certifications where applicable.

10. Push Notifications

If you grant notification permission, Jahera may send you bedtime story reminders at times you configure. You can withdraw this permission at any time through your device's notification settings. Disabling notifications does not affect any other App functionality.

The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
